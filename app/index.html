Hello!

The world we live in today is truly unlike anything history has ever seen.

We are witnesses to a golden age of technology every single day. The things we use are changing, adapting, and learning to fit our needs faster than we’re learning to use them. It’s not enough for digital products to just look nice anymore… They need to become an extension of ourselves, intrinsic to human nature itself.

I’m Christopher, a UX/UI Designer from Toronto, Ontario. I believe that design is the expression of human development - beautiful, but invisible. My passion isn’t creating beautiful experiences… It’s investigating and understanding people, and crafting these experiences around them. It’s with this approach that we find ourselves creating history; with every new whiteboard, wireframe, and mockup.

I’m excited to show you what I’ve been building as I continue my journey. I’m looking to join a team where I can continue to expand my knowledge and make meaningful contributions to an industry that fascinates and inspires me.
